## 🔐 Setting up your wallet

Initial setup requires attention to detail. You create or restore your wallet when you open the app for the first time.

* Create a new wallet: The app generates a secret recovery phrase. You must write this phrase on physical paper and store it in a safe place. Never digitize this phrase.
* Restore an existing wallet: Enter your secret recovery phrase into the appropriate fields to import your existing accounts.
* Password protection: Create a strong password to lock your local wallet data. This password prevents unauthorized access to the files on your computer.

## 🏠 Managing your assets

The interface displays your current balance clearly. You move between different networks with the dropdown menu at the top of the screen.

* Send funds: Click the send button and enter the recipient address. Confirm the transaction details before you hit submit.
* Receive funds: Select your public address to show a QR code or copy the string of characters. Share this address with those who wish to send assets to you.
* View history: The activity tab tracks your past transactions. Every entry shows the date, status, and associated tokens.

## 🛡️ Security best practices

Protecting your assets requires vigilance. Keep these rules in mind while you use the application.

* Never share your secret recovery phrase with anyone. No support representative will ask for it.
* Back up your recovery phrase in multiple secure locations.
* Use a firewall and antivirus software on your computer.
* Double check the web address of any site before you connect your wallet.
* Lock your computer when you walk away from your desk.
